About AUTOVOLT

Powering Innovation in Electric Mobility

Auto Volt Industries is a forward-thinking company dedicated to powering the future of mobility and energy. We specialize in manufacturing high-quality busbars, sheet metal enclosures, and thermal solutions that serve a wide range of industries, including electric vehicles, renewable energy, wind power, aerospace, and rail systems. With a strong reputation among top OEMs and Tier 1 suppliers, we are known for our precision engineering, reliable performance, and commitment to innovation.

Backed by a skilled in-house team and advanced manufacturing capabilities, we offer complete solutions from design and prototyping to full-scale production. Our focus on quality control at every stage, quick turnaround times, and just-in-time delivery makes us a trusted partner for clients looking for efficiency, customization, and long-term value.
